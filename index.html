CarbonBuddy

We can help you reduce your carbon footprint in 3 easy steps!



1. Take the quiz!

2. Answer honestly

3. Listen to the suggestions!


Information

Target audience: People interested in lowering their carbon footprint.

Goal: To help stop carbon pollution by making people more aware of their effects on the enviroment.

Description: CarbonBuddy is an application which gives you daily surveys about your carbon footprint. The app builds a profile based on your answers and gives you daily recommendations and tips to help lower your carbon footprint.

Our survey is on google classroom. Please fill it out and help us improve our product.
